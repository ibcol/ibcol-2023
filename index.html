International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is an annual competition inviting current and recent students to propose solutions to real-world problems enabled by blockchain technology

ABOUT

The international blockchain Olympiad is an annual student competition that challenges students to seek out a real-world problem that could be solved with a blockchain-enabled solution.
The objective of the competition is to provide students and young professionals for correct and complete blockchain solutions, the opportunity to showcase their knowledge and abilities in blockchain technology, gain valuable experience, and potentially win prizes or recognition for their work.
International Blockchain Olympiad was founded in 2017. Quietly growing into its 6th year, the International Blockchain Olympiad hosts its 2023 finals in Amsterdam.

WHY JOIN

- Future proof Skills for Self and Society

- Blockchain technology is a rapidly evolving field with the potential to revolutionize various industries and applications. As students, you have the opportunity to help shape this exciting and dynamic industry. In this competition, you can future-proof your skills and gain valuable experience in a field that is poised to grow rapidly in the coming years.
- Participating in this challenge is a great way to showcase your knowledge and abilities, and network with industry experts and professionals. Additionally, these events can help you stay up-to-date with the latest developments and best practices in the industry, and gain exposure to a wide range of blockchain projects and applications. So, if you're passionate about blockchain technology, don't hesitate to get involved and start making your mark in this exciting and innovative field!

- Certifications

- 20 teams will be shortlisted to proceed to the final round of competition hosted in Amsterdam. Top 3 will be presented with Gold, Silver and Bronze Medals. Distinction awards will be given to those who demonstrate exceptional knowledge and skills in blockchain technology, and merit awards will be given to those who show promise and potential in the field.
- If you have an idea, participating in the competition is a great way to present and exchange ideas, and potentially gaining recognition for your work!

Sponsors and Supporting Organisations

IBCOL offers a unique opportunity for students to showcase their skills and innovative ideas in the field of blockchain technology. IBCOL partners with academic institutions to provide access to facilities, resources, and expertise to help participants develop and refine their ideas. IBCOL also partners with companies to access funding, mentorship, and real-world insights to help participants turn their ideas into viable products or services. By working together, these partners have helped to support IBCOL to drive growth and promote the wider adoption of blockchain technology at international level.
